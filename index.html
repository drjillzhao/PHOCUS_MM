Ultra-low Energy Social Housing Delivery Toolkit

Maturity Self Assessment Toolkit and Action Plan for Social Housing Providers

This tool is co-developed by a Community of Practice (CoP), funded by the University of the West of England. Created by Jill Zhao. CoP members include: Phil Lewis, Mike Roe, Adam Harper, Primali Paranagamage, Jill Sanderson, Gillies MacPhail, Sarah Lewis, Matt Bridgestock, and Emma Osmundsen

This tool will help identify where you are on your journey to build more healthy and low-energy houses, and give you a range of steps to help you progress.

This tool is to be used by a housing development officer or someone with the equivalent knowledge and experience within a registered social housing provider organisation.
